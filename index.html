Welcome to Kajiado Parish Website

Located in the heart of Kenya, Kajiado Catholic Parish is a vibrant community dedicated to spreading the love and teachings of Jesus Christ. With a rich history rooted in faith and service, our parish warmly welcomes individuals from all walks of life to join us on our spiritual journey. Did you know that Kajiado Catholic Parish encompasses 19 outposts across the region? These outposts serve as beacons of hope and centers of worship for our diverse community members, bringing the message of faith and unity closer to home. Whether you're a long-time parishioner or visiting for the first time, all are welcome at Kajiado Catholic Parish. We invite you to join us in prayer, fellowship, and service as we strive to live out the Gospel values and make a positive difference in the lives of those around us. Come and experience the warmth of our community and the boundless love of God. We look forward to seeing you soon! God bless,
